FOOTBALL TRAINING DRILLS KNOWLEDGE BASE
==========================================

This knowledge base provides expert guidance for creating personalized football training plans based on player attributes, position, and performance statistics.

POSITION-SPECIFIC TRAINING FOCUS:
----------------------------------
- Forward: Shooting accuracy, finishing, first touch, movement in the box, positioning
- Midfielder: Passing accuracy, vision, ball control, tactical awareness, creativity
- Defender: Tackling technique, positioning, aerial ability, interception timing, distribution
- Goalkeeper: Shot-stopping, distribution, positioning, command of area, reflexes

ATTRIBUTE-BASED TRAINING RECOMMENDATIONS:
------------------------------------------

ATTACKING RATING:
- Low (<50): Focus on finishing drills, shooting technique, movement off the ball
- Medium (50-70): Improve shot selection, composure in front of goal, positioning
- High (>70): Maintain and refine, work on consistency and game situation training

TECHNIQUE RATING:
- Low (<50): Basic ball control drills, first touch exercises, technical fundamentals
- Medium (50-70): Advanced ball control, technical skills under pressure
- High (>70): Complex technical drills, game-realistic scenarios

CREATIVITY RATING:
- Low (<50): Decision-making drills, vision exercises, passing patterns
- Medium (50-70): Creative passing, through-ball practice, attacking combinations
- High (>70): Advanced playmaking, tactical creativity, game intelligence

TACTICAL RATING:
- Low (<50): Positional awareness, team shape, defensive positioning
- Medium (50-70): Tactical understanding, game reading, spatial awareness
- High (>70): Advanced tactics, leadership in positioning, game management

DEFENDING RATING:
- Low (<50): Basic tackling technique, positioning, marking, interception timing
- Medium (50-70): Advanced defending, pressing, recovery runs
- High (>70): Elite defending, anticipation, game reading

STATISTICS-BASED TRAINING PRIORITIES:
-------------------------------------

SHOOTING ACCURACY (shots_on_target_per_game / shots_per_game):
- Low (<50%): Target Shooting Drill, First Touch & Finish, Power Shot Training
- Medium (50-65%): Game Situation Shooting, Movement & Finish, Composure Training
- High (>65%): Advanced Finishing, Pressure Shooting, Game-Realistic Scenarios

PASSING ACCURACY (passes_completed / total_passes):
- Low (<80%): Basic Passing Patterns, Short Range Accuracy, First Touch Passing
- Medium (80-90%): Long Range Passing, Passing Under Pressure, Vision Drills
- High (>90%): Advanced Passing, Creative Passing, Game-Intelligence Passing

DRIBBLING SUCCESS (successful_dribbles / total_dribbles):
- Low (<60%): Basic Ball Control, 1v1 Situations, Close Control Drills
- Medium (60-75%): Advanced Dribbling, Dribbling Under Pressure, Speed Dribbling
- High (>75%): Elite Dribbling, Game-Realistic Dribbling, Creative Dribbling

TACKLING SUCCESS RATE:
- Low (<70%): Basic Tackling Technique, Timing Drills, Positioning for Tackles
- Medium (70-85%): Advanced Tackling, Recovery Tackles, Interception Training
- High (>85%): Elite Defending, Anticipation Training, Game-Reading Defending

INTERCEPTION SUCCESS RATE:
- Low (<70%): Positioning Drills, Reading the Game, Anticipation Training
- Medium (70-85%): Advanced Interceptions, Tactical Awareness, Spatial Recognition
- High (>85%): Elite Anticipation, Game Intelligence, Proactive Defending

RECOMMENDED DRILL TYPES BY WEAKNESS:
------------------------------------

SHOOTING ACCURACY ISSUES:
1. Target Shooting Drill - Practice shooting at designated target zones from various angles
2. First Touch & Finish - Receive passes and shoot with maximum 2 touches
3. Power Shot Training - Generate shot power while maintaining accuracy
4. Movement & Finish - Combine movement in the box with finishing
5. Composure Training - Finish under pressure and in game situations

PASSING ACCURACY ISSUES:
1. Short Range Passing Patterns - Basic accuracy drills at close range
2. Long Range Passing - Develop passing range and accuracy
3. Passing Under Pressure - Maintain accuracy when pressed
4. Vision Drills - Improve decision-making and passing selection
5. First Touch Passing - Quick, accurate passing after receiving

DRIBBLING SUCCESS ISSUES:
1. Basic Ball Control - Fundamental close control exercises
2. 1v1 Situations - Practice beating defenders one-on-one
3. Close Control Drills - Maintain possession in tight spaces
4. Dribbling Under Pressure - Dribble while being pressed
5. Speed Dribbling - Combine speed with control

DEFENDING ISSUES:
1. Basic Tackling Technique - Proper form and timing
2. Positioning Drills - Correct defensive positioning
3. Interception Training - Timing and anticipation
4. Recovery Runs - Getting back into position
5. Aerial Duels - Winning headers and clearances

TACTICAL AWARENESS ISSUES:
1. Positional Awareness - Understanding role and position
2. Team Shape - Maintaining formation and structure
3. Game Reading - Understanding game situations
4. Spatial Awareness - Understanding space and positioning
5. Decision Making - Making correct choices under pressure

TRAINING PLAN STRUCTURE:
-------------------------
- Duration: Typically 4-6 weeks for focused improvement
- Exercises: 3-5 specific drills per plan
- Sets: 3-4 sets per exercise
- Reps: 8-12 reps per set (or duration-based for continuous drills)
- Duration: 15-25 minutes per exercise

EXERCISE NAMING CONVENTIONS:
----------------------------
- Be specific and descriptive
- Include the skill being developed
- Examples: "Target Shooting Drill", "First Touch & Finish", "Power Shot Training"

EXERCISE DESCRIPTION GUIDELINES:
--------------------------------
- Clearly explain what the player will do
- Include key technical points
- Mention the objective or skill being developed
- Keep it concise but informative
